Community

We take pride in being everyone’s local chip shop and we try to give back where we can. Every year we sponsor Dunnington Cricket Club and we’ve also bought the footballs this season for Wilberfoss Mavericks U15’s.

We are also willing to serve for local events – we’ve catered for a wedding and many large functions. Get in touch if you’d like to discuss any local events that need feeding!
